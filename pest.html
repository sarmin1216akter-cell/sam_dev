üêû Common Agricultural Pests

Aphids

Small insects that suck plant sap and crops.

  • Affected Crops: Vegetables, fruits
  • Damage: Leaf curling, yellowing
  • Control: Neem oil, soap spray
Stem Borer

Larvae bore into stems causing plant death.

  • Affected Crops: Rice, maize
  • Damage: Dead hearts
  • Control: Pheromone traps
Whitefly

Transmits viral diseases and reduces crop yield.

  • Affected Crops: Cotton, tomato
  • Damage: Leaf discoloration
  • Control: Yellow sticky traps
